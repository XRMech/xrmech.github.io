Clint Campbell

I'm a software engineer with 12+ years of experience across mobile development, AI integration, mixed reality, full stack backend, and industrial automation.

I started at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory publishing peer-reviewed research on renewable energy land use, then spent 8 years at Boeing as a Robotics SME and programmer — earning a patent for a heat treatment process and leading the Boeing Robotics Community of Practice across three facilities.

Since 2021 I've been independent. I've shipped two cross-platform mobile apps to the Play Store and App Store, built XR tools for aerospace inspection and FDA-regulated medical devices, and taken on contract work through Arc.dev and directly. I build software that works under real constraints — production deployments, regulatory requirements, live users.

Currently based in Granada, Spain. Available remotely for clients in North America and Europe.
